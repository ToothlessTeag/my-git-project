About Tigers

Habitat

Tigers are native to Asia, and they are found in various regions including rainforests, grasslands, and swamps. They typically prefer dense forests where they can stalk their prey.

Diet

Tigers are carnivores, and their diet consists mainly of large mammals such as deer, wild boar, and even water buffalo. They are solitary hunters and rely on stealth to catch their prey.

Species of Tigers

  • Bengal Tiger
  • Siberian Tiger
  • Sumatran Tiger
  • Malayan Tiger
  • South China Tiger
